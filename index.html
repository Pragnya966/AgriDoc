Empowering farmers with intelligent crop care technology

What is AgriDoctor?

AgriDoctor is an AI-powered mobile-friendly web application designed to help farmers identify and treat crop diseases quickly and accurately. Using advanced image recognition, it analyzes photos of affected plants and provides instant, actionable diagnosis — even in regional languages like Telugu.

No agronomist needed. No expensive lab tests. Just take a photo and get your answer in seconds.
